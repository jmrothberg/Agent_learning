Build a Space Invaders game. Fixed shooter: A player cannon at the bottom moves left/right and fires upward at a grid of alien invaders that march side to side, dropping down and speeding up as their numbers thin. Aliens have a simple 2-frame animation (legs alternating). A few destructible bunkers shield the player, and occasionally a UFO crosses the top for bonus points. Aliens drop bombs. Controls: ArrowLeft/Right move, Space fire, R restart. Clear all aliens to advance the wave; an alien bomb hitting the player, or the aliens reaching the bottom, loses a life. HUD with score and lives. Classic arcade alien silhouettes (crab, squid, octopus rows) — NOT generic bugs. Crisp retro high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds.

Eat every dot to clear the level; a ghost catching the hero when not powered loses a life. HUD shows score and lives. Colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds, dark maze walls. Expose collectiblesLeft or dotsRemaining on window.state.

Colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds. Generated PNGs → drawImage. Hop one cell per keydown edge (not key-hold spam).
