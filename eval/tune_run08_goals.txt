Build a Doom game. First-person raycaster shooter (or three.js): The player walks through a textured maze of walls, floor, and ceiling with mouse-look (pointer-lock) and WASD movement, shooting monsters that approach as billboard sprites always facing the camera. The level map must have visible walls (not HUD-only empty scene); state.map or state.maze must be non-empty and enemies[] must spawn monsters. Each monster needs an idle frame and an attack/hit frame; the first-person weapon is a screen-space overlay with idle and muzzle-flash firing frames (the muzzle points UP in the source art). A crosshair sits at screen center and the camera does all the aiming. Controls: WASD move, mouse look, click or Space fire, R restart. Pick up health and ammo; clear all monsters to finish the level, dying at zero health. HUD with health, ammo, and score. Generate VIDEO CUTSCENES for a level-intro establishing shot (corridor or hellscape) and a boss-reveal clip before the final encounter (seeded from key-art stills via image-to-video); muted skippable full-screen overlays, FPS gameplay stays in the raycaster. High-resolution detailed wall textures and monster sprites, transparent backgrounds on the sprites, plus gunfire/explosion SFX and ominous looping music. RE-RUN — validates 3D FPS navigation fix (2026-06-30). Movement MUST use camera-relative vectors (applyQuaternion(camera.quaternion)) or fx=-Math.sin(yaw) everywhere — NOT fx=+Math.sin(yaw) with camera.rotation.y=yaw.
Build a Minecraft game. First-person voxel sandbox with three.js: The player explores a 3D world of cube blocks (grass, dirt, stone — each a distinct named block type in state.hotbar) on chunked terrain, with mouse-look (pointer-lock) and WASD movement plus space-to-jump under gravity. Left-click breaks the targeted block and right-click places the selected block; raycast from the camera to highlight the block face under the crosshair. A small hotbar selects the block type. Controls: WASD move, Space jump, mouse look, left/right click break/place, number keys select block, R restart. High-resolution blocky voxel textures, a bright sky, and simple ambient lighting. Expose the world state for verification.
Build a Space Invaders game. Fixed shooter: A player cannon at the bottom moves left/right and fires upward at a grid of alien invaders that march side to side, dropping down and speeding up as their numbers thin. Aliens have a simple 2-frame animation (legs alternating). A few destructible bunkers shield the player, and occasionally a UFO crosses the top for bonus points. Aliens drop bombs. Controls: ArrowLeft/Right move, Space fire, R restart. Clear all aliens to advance the wave; an alien bomb hitting the player, or the aliens reaching the bottom, loses a life. HUD with score and lives. Classic arcade alien silhouettes (crab, squid, octopus rows) — NOT generic bugs. Crisp retro high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds.
Build a Dig Dug game. A digger moves through solid earth, carving tunnels in any of four directions, and fights underground monsters by pumping them up with an air harpoon until they pop, or by dropping rocks on them. Solid rock tiles must block movement — the digger cannot pass through undug earth or rocks. The digger needs idle, dig (per direction), and pump (arm extending the harpoon) frames; monsters have a walk and an inflating animation. Controls: Arrow keys move/dig, Space pump, R restart. Clear all monsters to advance the level; a monster touching the digger loses a life. HUD with score and lives. Colorful high-resolution sprites, layered soil colors, transparent backgrounds.
Build a Bomberman game. Grid game: A character moves on a tile grid and drops bombs that explode in a plus-shaped blast after a short fuse, destroying soft blocks (which sometimes reveal power-ups: more bombs, longer blast range) and any enemy or player caught in the flames. The hero needs idle and walk frames per direction. Simple enemies wander the maze. Controls: Arrow keys move, Space drop bomb, R restart. Clear all enemies and reach the exit to advance; being caught in any blast or touched by an enemy loses a life. HUD with score and lives. Colorful high-resolution top-down sprites, transparent backgrounds.
