Build a Donkey Kong game. Single-screen arcade platformer: A plumber hero climbs a stack of sloped girders connected by ladders to rescue a captured figure at the top, while a big ape rolls barrels down toward him. The hero is a detailed character needing animated poses from idle — idle, run (legs cycling), climb (arms reaching up a ladder), jump (legs tucked mid-leap), and a hit/fall — at least 2 frames from idle for each. BARRELS: each barrel MUST stay on the girder surface and ROLL DOWN the slope (follow platform yAt(x) while moving toward the low end). At the low end / ladder gap it TUMBLES down onto the next girder, reverses direction, and keeps rolling — never free-fall off the first floor into empty air or straight to the bottom. Jumping over a barrel scores points; touching one loses a life. Controls: ArrowLeft/Right run, ArrowUp/Down climb ladders, Space jump, R restart. Reach the top to win the level; lose all lives to game over. Colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds. Generated PNGs must be drawn with drawImage (not fillRect placeholders). Barrel hazard loop must keep spawning — do not one-shot spawn.
Build a Frogger game. Crossing game: A frog starts at the bottom and must cross a busy multi-lane road (cars and trucks moving at different speeds and directions) then a river (hopping on floating logs and turtles; water is deadly) to reach home slots at the top. The frog hops one grid cell per key press and needs idle and mid-hop (legs extended) frames facing its move direction. Controls: Arrow keys hop, R restart. Filling all home slots wins the level; getting hit by traffic, falling in the water, or running out of time loses a life. HUD with score, lives, and a timer. Colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds. Generated PNGs → drawImage. Hop one cell per keydown edge (not key-hold spam).
Build a Dragon's Lair game. Laserdisc QTE: scripted peril scenes, timed key reactions, hazards drawn ON TOP of full-screen backgrounds. USER GOAL WINS — read the goal first: • Scene count: if the user asks for 10 scenes (or 5, or 12), plan and ship that many — do NOT cap at 8. • Look and characters: if the user asks for cartoon style, pixel art, different heroes, princess-led, etc., use THAT in every <assets>/<videos> prompt — the default cel-animation knight below is only a fallback when the goal is silent. • Perils and setting: swap, add, or reorder rooms to match the goal; keep the same QTE mechanics. VISUAL RULE (always — every scene, any count, any art style): Three layers each frame: (1) static bg_* stage, (2) hazard sprite that MOVES toward the body part about to get hit, (3) hero sprite on top. The player reads the PICTURE to know when to act; the centered word ('JUMP!', 'DUCK!') only says WHICH key. Duck = threat drops toward the head; jump = gap/ledge moves to the feet; dodge = threat sweeps from that side; sword = lunge at torso. DEFAULT 8-SCENE TEMPLATE (1983 arcade reference — use when the goal does not specify scenes; otherwise adapt, extend, or replace): 1 Drawbridge (bg_drawbridge) — JUMP: planks crack, gap at feet. 2 Slime hall (bg_tentacle_hall) — SWORD: tentacle lunges from pit. 3 Blade corridor (bg_blade_corridor) — DUCK then JUMP: blade at head, then low blade at legs. 4 Crumbling stairs (bg_crumbling_stairs) — RIGHT then JUMP: rubble from left, abyss below. 5 Bat cavern (bg_bat_cavern) — SWORD then DUCK: swarm, then dive at head. 6 Lava ledge (bg_lava_ledge) — LEFT then JUMP: fireball from right, ledge cracks. 7 Throne antechamber (bg_throne_hall) — RIGHT then SWORD: arch falls, phantom flame. 8 Dragon's lair (bg_dragons_lair) — LEFT then SWORD: dragon fire, jaws snap. (Add scenes 9–10+ with the same telegraph pattern when the goal asks for more.) PHASE A: same reply must include <plan>, <criteria>, <probes>, <assets>, <sounds>, <videos> so the pipeline runs up front. Criteria must match YOUR scene list (not necessarily 8). MEDIA: ONE canonical hero description from the goal's style — reuse VERBATIM on every hero sprite and key-art prompt (change ONLY pose words). bg_* are character-free stages. Count bg_* and hero_* / knight_* pose sprites to match your scene list. Three muted I2V videos (intro, fail, victory) from key_* stills unless the goal says otherwise. CODE: SCENES array sized to your plan; drawImage(bg) → animate hazard → hero pose; performance.now() windows; 0-index state.scene; window.state + window.game.reset; on keydown set lastInputCode/inputFlash; onended/onerror always continues; requestAnimationFrame(frame) immediately; tolerate missing media. Single HTML file.
