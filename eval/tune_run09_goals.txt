Build a Centipede game. Fixed shooter: The player controls a blaster at the bottom that moves freely in the lower zone and fires upward. When a shot hits a body segment it leaves a mushroom. The blaster needs idle and firing frames; the centipede head and body are sprites. Controls: Arrow keys move, Space fire, R restart. Crisp colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds.
