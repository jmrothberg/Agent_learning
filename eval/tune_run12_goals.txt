Build a Prince of Persia game. Cinematic side-view platformer in a dungeon: a lone hero runs, does a measured running-jump across gaps, hangs from and climbs up ledges, and crosses hazards (spikes, a slicing blade, a loose floor tile that drops). The hero is a detailed rotoscoped character needing fluid animated poses from idle — idle, walk/run cycle (legs cycling), running-jump (reach + tuck), ledge-hang and pull-up, and a sword stance/strike — at least 2 frames from idle for each so motion reads as weighty and realistic. A guard blocks the path: draw sword (Shift), then attack/parry to defeat it. Controls: ArrowLeft/Right walk/run, ArrowUp climb/pull-up, Space jump, Shift draw sword, R restart. Reach the exit door to win; falling into a pit, spikes, or losing the swordfight loses a life. HUD with a countdown timer and lives. Generate VIDEO CUTSCENES for a title/intro before the level and a victory scene at the exit door (each seeded from key-art stills via image-to-video); muted skippable full-screen overlays, platforming stays on the canvas. High-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds, plus footstep/sword SFX and tense looping music.
Build a Metal Slug run-and-gun game. Side-scrolling action platformer: a commando runs and jumps across a scrolling battlefield of platforms and sandbags while firing a rapid gun at waves of soldiers and a heavy tank/turret. Aim while shooting — fire straight ahead by facing, straight up with ArrowUp, or diagonally when a direction and up are held; you can shoot while standing, running, or jumping. The commando is a detailed character needing animated poses from idle — idle, run cycle, jump, and a shoot/recoil pose — at least 2 frames from idle for each; enemies have a hit/explode animation. Grab a weapon pickup for a spread/heavy shot. Controls: ArrowLeft/Right run, Space jump, X or Z fire, ArrowUp aim up, R restart. Clear the enemies and reach the end to win; taking a hit loses a life. HUD with score, ammo, and lives. Crisp colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds, punchy gunfire/explosion SFX and driving music.
Build a Mortal Kombat fighting game. Two fighters face each other on a single stage, one on the left and one on the right, each with a health bar at the top. Each fighter is a detailed character needing animated poses from idle — idle stance (breathing bob), walk toward/away, high punch, low kick, block, a projectile special (e.g. thrown fireball), and a hit/stagger — at least 2 frames from idle for each; fighters always turn to FACE each other. Controls (P1): ArrowLeft/Right move, ArrowUp jump, ArrowDown crouch/block, A punch, S kick, D special; the CPU opponent reacts and attacks. Drain the opponent's health to win the round; best of the bout. When a fighter is finished, trigger a FATALITY: generate a VIDEO CUTSCENE finisher/victory (seeded from key-art stills via image-to-video) as a muted skippable full-screen overlay, then return to the reset() path for a rematch. HUD with two health bars and a round timer. High-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds, impactful hit SFX and stage music.
Build a Rampage game. A giant monster clings to the face of a tall city skyscraper and climbs it, punching windows and floors to smash the building to rubble while dodging helicopters and gunfire. The monster is a detailed character needing animated poses from idle — cling/idle, climb cycle (limbs reaching up the wall), a punch/smash swing, eat-from-window, and a hit/recoil — at least 2 frames from idle for each; smashed floors visibly crumble and the building shrinks. Grab civilians/food from windows to recover health. Controls: ArrowUp/Down climb, ArrowLeft/Right move across the facade, Space punch/smash, R restart. Demolish the building to win the level (a taller one appears); losing all health from helicopters/gunfire ends the rampage. HUD with score and a health/damage meter. Bold colorful high-resolution sprites, transparent backgrounds, crunchy smash/explosion SFX and a rampaging soundtrack.
